Description

A creamy, sweet drink made from ripe bananas - perfect for breakfast or a quick snack. Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup of milk (or water)
  • 1 tbsp honey or sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Steps

  1. Peel and cut bananas into small pieces.
  2. Put bananas, milk, and honey in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Add ice cubes and blend again if you want it cold.
  5. Pour into a cup and enjoy.
