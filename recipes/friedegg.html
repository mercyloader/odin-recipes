Fried Eggs with Vegetables

Home

Description

A quick breakfast or light lunch - fluffy eggs with colourful, slightly crunchy veggies for extra taste and nutrition.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 small onion (chopped)
  • 1/2 tomato (chopped)
  • 1/4 green pepper (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil or butter
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of black pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper, and whisk lightly.
  2. Heat oil/butter in a pan on medium heat.
  3. Add onions, stir for 1-2 minutes
  4. Add tomatoes and green pepper, cook another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Pour in the whisked eggs, let cook for a few seconds, then stir gently until set.
  6. Serve hot with bread or rice.
